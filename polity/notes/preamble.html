✦ The Preamble — Text

WE, THE PEOPLE OF INDIA, having solemnly resolved to constitute India into a SOVEREIGN SOCIALIST SECULAR DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC and to secure to all its citizens: JUSTICE, Social, Economic and Political; LIBERTY of thought, expression, belief, faith and worship; EQUALITY of status and of opportunity; and to promote among them all FRATERNITY assuring the dignity of the individual and the UNITY AND INTEGRITY OF THE NATION; IN OUR CONSTITUENT ASSEMBLY this twenty-sixth day of November, 1949, do HEREBY ADOPT, ENACT AND GIVE TO OURSELVES THIS CONSTITUTION.

1 What Is the Preamble?
📖

Philosophy of the Constitution

The Preamble is the soul and core idea of the Constitution — like a preface to a book, it reveals what the Constitution is all about.

🔑

Key to Makers' Minds

Berubari Union Case (1960): The Preamble is the "key to unlock the mind of the makers" of the Constitution (Constituent Assembly).

⚖️

Ideals & Aspirations

Supreme Court described the Preamble as containing the ideals and aspirations of the Constitution — its guiding spirit.

2 Source of the Preamble
Origin

Objectives Resolution by Pandit Nehru

The Preamble is based on the Objectives Resolution moved by Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru in the Constituent Assembly and adopted on 22nd January 1947. With some changes in wording, the Objectives Resolution became the Preamble.

3 Four Broad Themes
Theme 1 — Source of Authority

"We the People of India"

The Constitution derives its authority from the people of India — not from any foreign power (UN, UK, USA, etc.). It is the gift of the people to themselves. This reflects Popular Sovereignty — people are the ultimate sovereign in a democracy.

Theme 2 — Nature of the Indian State

"Sovereign Socialist Secular Democratic Republic"

All terms are written in capital letters without commas → they form one integrated whole.

Sovereign Socialist ★ Secular ★ Democratic Republic

★ Added by 42nd Constitutional Amendment Act, 1976

🛡️ Sovereign

India can make/unmake any decision without external interference. Joining WTO was voluntary — sovereignty is not compromised.

🗳️ Democratic

Government of the people, by the people, for the people (Abraham Lincoln). Equal participation; right to vote and contest.

🏛️ Republic

Head of State (President) is elected, not hereditary (unlike Britain's constitutional monarchy). Office open to all citizens without discrimination.

⚡ Socialist (42nd Amdt)

Fabian/Gandhian type — Welfare State. Not Marxist (no abolition of private property). Aim: minimize inequality. D.S. Nakara vs UoI.

🕌 Secular (42nd Amdt)

Principled Distance / Positive Secularism. State equally protects all religions. No state religion. Arts. 15, 16, 27. Before 1976: implicit; after 1976: explicit.

Theme 3 — Objectives of the Constitution

Justice · Liberty · Equality · Fraternity

⚖️ Justice

Social: End caste/gender discrimination, untouchability, manual scavenging.
Economic: Reduce inequality; MGNREGA, scholarships, pensions.
Political: Universal Adult Franchise; Lily Thomas (2013) — struck down Sec. 8(4) RP Act.

🕊️ Liberty

Freedom of thought, expression, belief, faith, and worship. Protects individuals from arbitrary state interference.

🟰 Equality

No discrimination on grounds of religion, race, caste, sex, or place of birth. Equal status and opportunity for all citizens.

🤝 Fraternity

Sense of brotherhood despite diversity in language, religion, caste, food, dress. Promotes Unity in Diversity.

🇫🇷 Borrowed from French Revolution: Liberty, Equality, Fraternity | "Integrity" added by 42nd Amendment to counter secessionist tendencies (Khalistan, Kashmir, Northeast movements).

Theme 4 — Date of Adoption

26th November 1949

📅 Constitution Day (Samvidhan Divas)

Adopted on 26 November 1949. Celebrated as Constitution Day / Samvidhan Divas.

🗓️ Republic Day

Enacted/came into force on 26 January 1950. Chose this date to link with the Lahore Session (1929) — Purna Swaraj resolution and first Independence Day celebration on 26 Jan 1930.

4 Supreme Court Observations
1960

Berubari Union Case

Preamble is not part of the Constitution. It is the "key to unlock the mind of the makers." Preamble cannot grant powers or impose restrictions by itself.

1973

Kesavananda Bharati Case

Overruled Berubari — Preamble is part of the Constitution. Preamble contains elements of the Basic Structure. Parliament can amend the Preamble to enrich it but cannot damage the basic structure.

1995

LIC of India Case

Reaffirmed Kesavananda Bharati — Preamble is part of the Constitution. It helps interpret ambiguous constitutional provisions.

2017

Justice K.S. Puttaswamy Case

Right to Privacy under Article 21 was derived partly using "Dignity" from the Preamble — demonstrating its interpretive role in constitutional jurisprudence.

5 42nd Constitutional Amendment, 1976

⚡ Words Added to the Preamble

During the Emergency (Indira Gandhi era), the 42nd Constitutional Amendment Act, 1976 added three words to the Preamble:

Socialist Secular Integrity

Before 1976, Secularism was implicit in the Constitution; after 1976 it became explicit. "Integrity" was added to counter growing secessionist tendencies in certain states.

✦ Quick One-Liner Summary

The Preamble is the soul and philosophy of the Indian Constitution. It declares India as a Sovereign Socialist Secular Democratic Republic and outlines the objectives of securing Justice, Liberty, Equality, and Fraternity while assuring the dignity of the individual and the unity and integrity of the Nation. It derives its authority from "We the People of India" and was adopted on 26 November 1949.
