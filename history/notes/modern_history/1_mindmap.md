## ⏳ UNIT 1 — TIMELINE: Decline of Mughal Empire (1707–1857)

```mermaid
timeline
    title Mughal Empire — Decline to Disappearance (150 Years)
    1707 : Death of Aurangzeb
         : Decline of Mughals begins
         : Political & economic weaknesses surface
    1712 : Death of Bahadur Shah I
         : Era of powerful nobles begins
         : Emperor becomes puppet of nobles
    1713–1719 : Jahandar Shah & Farrukh Siyar
              : Sayyid Brothers become Kingmakers
              : Nobles control throne
    1720 : End of Sayyid Brothers
         : Muhammad Shah Rangila rises
         : Nizam-ul-Mulk appointed Wazir
    1724 : Hyderabad founded — first breakup
         : Nizam-ul-Mulk declares independence
         : Physical disintegration starts
    1739 : Nadir Shah invades — Battle of Karnal
         : Peacock Throne & Koh-i-Noor looted
         : 70 crore rupees taken away
    1750s : Full disintegration
          : Bengal, Awadh become independent
          : Marathas & British race for power
    1803 : Delhi captured by British
         : Mughal Emperor becomes pensioner
         : Sovereignty lost completely
    1857 : Revolt of 1857
         : Bahadur Shah Zafar II leads revolt
         : Mughal Empire officially ends
```
